## Comparative Analysis
All three AI tools performed well, but they had different strengths. Gemini Flash was
consistently fast and useful for quick answers, but sometimes lacked depth. Claude
provided more natural language and thoughtful explanations, especially for creative
and reflective tasks.

ChatGPT stood out for structured problem-solving and consistency. It handled complex
tasks like planning and comparisons very well, making it the best overall choice for
academic work.

## My Choice for Primary Tool
**Tool**: ChatGPT  
**Reasoning**: Best balance of speed, clarity, and structure  
**Backup Plan**: Use Claude when hitting rate limits
