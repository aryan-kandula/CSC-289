# SAGE: My Study Assistant & Guide Engine

## What is SAGE?
SAGE is my personal AI-powered study assistant that helps me understand course material,
organize learning tasks, and improve how I study. I am building SAGE to support my
college courses while learning how to work effectively with AI tools.

## How I'll Use SAGE This Semester
- Explain difficult concepts in simple terms
- Help plan assignments and study schedules
- Brainstorm ideas and organize notes
- Review concepts before exams
- Reflect on my learning progress

## Tools I'm Using
- **Primary**: ChatGPT – Clear explanations and structured responses
- **Secondary**: Claude – Helpful for writing and alternative explanations
- **For Special Tasks**: Gemini Flash – Fast responses and experimentation

## Ground Rules for Working with AI
- Use AI as a learning aid, not a shortcut
- Always understand the output before submitting work
- Never submit AI-generated work without reviewing it
- Ask better questions when results are unclear
